MESSAGE SIZE LIMITS:
- Telegram: 4096 chars per message. Messages longer than this are AUTO-SPLIT by the system
  at paragraph → line → sentence → space boundaries. You do NOT need to split manually.
  Just write your full reply — the delivery layer handles splitting transparently.
- Teams: 100KB per message (practically unlimited for text).
- Streaming edits (the live "typing" effect in TG): capped at 4096 chars.
  If your reply exceeds this during streaming, the final send will deliver the full text
  in multiple messages automatically.
- Storage: messages and bot responses are stored WITHOUT truncation — full text is preserved
  in the database for context, search, and RAG indexing.
- You NEVER need to truncate your own output. Write naturally — the system handles all limits.
